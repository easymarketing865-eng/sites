# Visual Style Guide

## Overview

This document describes the visual style observed in the "easy. production" creative agency presentations.

---

## 1. Typography

- **Headlines:**  
  - Extremely bold, uppercase sans-serif font.
  - Deep red color (#A00000 or similar).
  - Used for maximum impact and clear hierarchy.

- **Supporting Text:**  
  - All-caps, black, smaller size.
  - Clean, modern sans-serif font.
  - Used for descriptions, navigation, and secondary information.

- **Accent Font:**  
  - Handwritten script font for select words (e.g., "creative").
  - Adds a dynamic, personal, and creative touch.

---

## 2. Color Palette

- **Primary:** Deep red (for headlines and accents).
- **Secondary:** Black (for body and supporting text).
- **Background:** White or very light beige for high contrast and clarity.

---

## 3. Layout

- **Structure:**  
  - Clean, minimalist, grid-based.
  - Large headlines aligned left.
  - Supporting text and images arranged in a structured grid.
  - Ample white space for a modern, uncluttered look.

- **Spacing:**  
  - Generous padding and margins.
  - Clear separation between sections and elements.

---

## 4. Imagery

- **Style:**  
  - Candid, documentary-style photos.
  - Real people, real environments, authentic moments.
  - Images grouped in a grid, reinforcing structure and professionalism.

- **Presentation:**  
  - Photos are vibrant and high-quality.
  - Used to support and enhance the message, not distract.

---

## 5. Branding

- **Logo:**  
  - Consistent use of the "easy. production" logo in the top left.
- **Consistency:**  
  - Repeated visual motifs and color usage across all slides.
  - Strong, unified brand identity.

---

## 6. Editorial Feel

- **Modern, Magazine-like:**  
  - Bold editorial typography.
  - Script accents for personality.
  - Documentary photography for authenticity.
  - Clear visual hierarchy.

---

## 7. Navigation/Presentation Thumbnails

- **Thumbnails:**  
  - Small previews of other slides or presentations at the bottom.
  - Consistent style and clear labeling.
  - Helps users navigate and understand the breadth of work.

---

## Summary

The visual style is bold, modern, and editorial, with a strong emphasis on typography, minimalism, and authentic imagery. It communicates creativity, confidence, and professionalism, and ensures a consistent, memorable brand experience.
