# easy. production — Mobile-First Landing Page & Marketing Blueprint

This plan outlines a world-class, mobile-first website and marketing strategy for easy. production, blending bold editorial design, authentic imagery, and international marketing best practices. The structure below follows the preferred order from the latest paper sketch, ensuring a direct, impactful user journey.

---

## 1. Brand & Visual Approach

- **Editorial, Modern, Bold:**  
  - Large, uppercase headlines in deep red (#A00000).
  - Clean, all-caps supporting text in black.
  - Handwritten script accents for creative energy (e.g., "creative", "production", "location").
- **Imagery:**  
  - Candid, documentary-style photos and vibrant project visuals.
  - Grid/collage layouts for authenticity and structure.
- **Color Palette:**  
  - Deep red, black, white/light beige backgrounds for clarity and impact.
- **Layout:**  
  - Minimalist, grid-based, with generous white space and clear hierarchy.
- **Mobile-First:**  
  - All elements scale gracefully, with touch-friendly navigation and swipeable carousels.

---

## 2. Landing Page Structure (User-Preferred Order)

### 1. Hero Headshot Section
- **Logo:** Top left, always visible.
- **Headline:** Large, bold, uppercase (e.g., "WORLD-CLASS VIDEO PRODUCTION").
- **Accent Script:** Handwritten overlay (e.g., "Ycayzu" or "creative").
- **Hero Image:** Full-width, high-impact photo (from `photos/headshot.webp`).
- **Short Tagline:** One sentence about the agency’s mission or vibe.

### 2. Service/Feature Cards (Row of Four)
- "Ycayzu" (Creative Direction)
- "Brand" (Brand Strategy)
- "Script Writing" (Story & Scripting)
- "Broadcast & Creativity" (Media & Production)
- **Each card:** Icon/image, bold title, short description.

### 3. Showreel Section
- **Background:**  
  - Full-section overlay of handwritten-style words (e.g., production, scriptwriting, location, creativity, direction, editing, storytelling, filming, Kyrgyzstan, team, value, quality, Dubai, visuals, documentary, commercial, brand, campaign, crew, post-production, vision, authenticity, cinematic, global, cost-effective, unique, experience, support, partnership, innovation, impact, strategy, collaboration, expertise, flexibility, reliability, trust, scale, international, Central Asia, permits, logistics, equipment, 
  , RED, drone, lighting, sound, talent, story, concept, execution, result, success, client, agency, brand, filmmaker, project, showcase, portfolio, testimonial, case study, location, mountain, lake, city, steppe, culture, architecture).
  - Handwritten script font, deep red, low opacity for subtlety.
- **Showreel Video:**  
  - Prominently featured, with poster image.
- **Quick Facts:**  
  - Gear, ARRI, Scripts, etc.

### 4. Trusted Brands / Bento Grid
- **Section Title:** "Best Brands Trust Us"
- **Grid of Client Logos/Images:**

### 5. We Cater To (Target Segments)
- **Section Title:** "We Cater To"
- **Columns for Each Segment:**  
  - **Independent Filmmakers:** High production value, unique aesthetics, cost-effective, creative, visually striking Kyrgyz locations.

## 4. Content & Messaging Strategy

- **Unique Selling Propositions (USPs):**
  - High-Quality Production Standards: International benchmarks, technical expertise, creative vision, state-of-the-art equipment.
  - Significant Cost-Effectiveness: Kyrgyzstan’s cost advantage, value without sacrificing quality.
  - Unique & Scenic Locations: Mountains, lakes, Soviet-era architecture, vibrant culture, easy permits.
  - Proven International Experience: Dubai project as social proof, global standards, cross-cultural collaboration.
  - Large Team Capacity: Scalable, skilled crew for any project size.

- **Portfolio Sneak Peek:**  
  - Thumbnails or short clips from standout projects, including Dubai.

- **Location Teaser:**  
  - Visual highlight of Kyrgyzstan’s unique filming opportunities.

- **Social Proof:**  
  - Client logos, short testimonials, case studies.

- **Clear CTAs:**  
  - "Explore Our Work", "Discover Locations", "Get a Quote".
